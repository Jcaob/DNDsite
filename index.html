    Welcome To Igrim

    What is Igrim

    Igrim is a Fantasy World made for Dungeons and Dragons where
    Players can roam free and attempt to fight back the evil or join it.

    Gods

    The lands of Igrim host 6 gods residing in a place beyond mortal perception. some good, some evil, some just plain chaotic.
    While serving a god isnt required in this world avoiding their influence will be impossible.
    So will you serve?
